About NLQ Assistant

Democratizing data access through natural language

Our Mission

We believe that data insights should be accessible to everyone, regardless of technical expertise. Our mission is to bridge the gap between complex databases and human curiosity by enabling natural language queries to data.

NLQ Assistant transforms how people interact with data—no SQL required, just plain English questions that get instant answers.

Why We Built This

Traditional BI tools and database queries require extensive training. We wanted to make it simple— ask a question, get an answer, no steep learning curve.
